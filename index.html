Academics

We have Math, Science, World Cultures, English, and three world language options: Spanish, French, and Mandarin. We also have Related Arts: Art, Drama, Engineering, Music, Physical Education, and Media Arts.



Extracurricular

Students have options to join clubs and sports. We offer Basketball, Soccer, Volleyball, Swimming, and Lacrosse. Our clubs include Art, drama, band, homework, boardgames, and much more. Also, new clubs are being formed each year.
